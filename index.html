How it Works

Four steps to deep focus

A simple workflow designed to get you into the zone and keep you there.

01

Install in seconds

Add Focus Flow to your browser in one click. No account needed — it works right away.

02

Start a session

Set your focus duration, choose allowed sites, and hit start. Distractions are blocked immediately.

03

Stay in flow

Social media and entertainment sites are blocked. Only your approved sites stay accessible.

04

Track your progress

Review focus time, streaks, and blocked distractions to build better habits over time.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Simple and powerful — no bloat, no subscriptions.

Focus Timer

Set custom sessions with built-in breaks. Locks your session so you stay on track until time is up.

Smart Site Blocking

Block entire categories or specific URLs. Only approved sites remain during a session.

Productivity Dashboard

View your daily and weekly focus time, session history, and improvement trends.

Streak Tracking

Build momentum with daily streaks. Complete a session each day to keep your streak alive.

Break Reminders

Focus Flow tells you when to rest and when to return. Rest is part of the system.

Calm Focus Mode

A minimal overlay that reduces visual noise and keeps your browser distraction-free.
